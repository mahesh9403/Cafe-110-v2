001 / Our Story

From the very beginning.

Food has always been a supporting character at the centre of our dinner table, friends and family sitting together and talking away. From silly jokes to opening up on our deepest darkest secrets, our dinner table has heard it all. We grew up around the table and it has seen us through all stages of life.

From some of our firsts to some of our lasts, the table has always been a constant observer open to all and our life has evolved around it. Our door is always open, strangers walk in and become family, family walk away and become strangers and life goes full circle.

This is what OneTen is, not just an extension of our family's house number but an invitation to our dinner table. When you walk in through the door, you're already someone we want to sit and share a meal with. Spend some time with us and in return we'll share our table with you.

If nothing else, thank you for walking in.

With love,

003 / The Space

Built from the ground up.
Intentional corners all around.

The very first impression of OneTen is the building itself. Nestled between large trees, the entrance is filled with plants with white marble stairs leading to our beloved wooden arched door that feels like you enter into a portal. As soon as you enter, our Barista's big bright smile welcomes you in asking you to make way to the stairs leading up to the first floor. The walk up the stairs is always magical, this is where most of the pictures are taken. The first floor has two seating areas, the indoor and the Gazebo. Both spaces with energy that slows you down ready to savour your first bite.

Green Earthy Entrance Entrance lined with plants and earthy wooden tones — infusing nature into the space.
Lamps That Scatter Shadows An enchanting walk up and down the stairs.
Gazebo Dine under the Hyderabad sky. Open air, glass enclosed.
Candle Lit Tables & Hand-Crafted Flower Lamps Soft, sweet lighting in the evening that provides a canvas for beautiful pictures.
Intentional Music Always in the background, soothing and calming. Loud enough to heal, soft enough to hear your friends.
