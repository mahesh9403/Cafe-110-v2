Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

What data we collect

Cafe OneTen's website does not collect, store, or process any personal data. There are no user accounts, no sign-up forms, and no contact forms on this site. Visiting any page on this site does not create a record of your identity.

Cookies

This website does not set any cookies. No tracking, advertising, or analytics cookies are used. Your browser will not receive any cookies from cafeoneten.com.

Third-party services

This site links to and embeds services provided by third parties. Each operates under its own privacy policy:

  • Google Maps — The Visit page embeds a Google Maps iframe. Google may collect data when the map loads. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • WhatsApp — Reservation links open WhatsApp (wa.me). WhatsApp processes any message you send. See WhatsApp's Privacy Policy.
  • Zomato, Swiggy Dineout, EazyDiner — Reservation buttons link to these external platforms. Their own privacy policies apply if you visit or book through them.
  • Instagram — The footer links to our Instagram profile. Instagram's privacy policy applies if you follow the link.

Server logs

Like any website, our hosting provider (Hostinger) may record standard server access logs — IP address, page requested, time, and browser type — for security and uptime purposes. These logs are not used for marketing and are not shared with third parties.

Your rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete. If you have any privacy-related questions, contact us at hello@cafeoneten.com.
